About BCCL

With decades of experience in the coal mining industry, BCCL has established itself as a leader in coking coal production in India.

Our Journey

Founded in 1971, Bharat Coking Coal Limited has grown to become one of the largest coking coal producers in India. Our operations span multiple mines across Jharkhand, employing state-of-the-art technology and sustainable mining practices.

We are committed to operational excellence, safety standards, and environmental responsibility while meeting the nation's growing energy demands.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted and innovative coal mining company, delivering sustainable energy solutions while prioritizing safety, environmental conservation, and community welfare.
